Goal of the Game

This is a VIRTUAL ESCAPE ROOM, a perfect puzzle solving game for quarantine. The game involves two parties:

  1. An ESCAPEE, who needs to ESCAPE THE DEVICE before time runs out
  2. The INSTRUCTORS, who need to tell the escapee how to escape the device

The goal is for the instructors to give the escapee the needed steps to ESCAPE THE DEVICE successfully before TIME RUNS OUT! The escapee will initialize the game with his device, and the instructors will be sent instructions on how to escape. The instructors need to COMMUNICATE to the escapee how to escape the device. The escapee will NOT HAVE THE INSTRUCTIONS and the instructions the instructors will receive will be all JUMBLED UP.
